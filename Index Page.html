Online Vegetable Seeds Management

Welcome to the online vegetable seeds management system designed to help a big farm to manage their vegetable seeds bank. The system will perform the following functionalities:

  • Users search for seeds by their name, category, planting time and expire date
  • System will automatically check what vegetable should be planted in the month
  • System will send an automatically alert email to administrator if seed is less than 500g or if expiry date of seeds is less than 1 year
  • Administrator can generate yearly report on the top 5 harvested seeds and top 5 wasted seeds
